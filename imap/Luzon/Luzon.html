Kapurpurawan Rock Formation,Pagudpud, Ilocos Norte:

This geological marvel features unique rock formations that resemble mushrooms, hence the name "Kapurpurawan" which translates to "whimsical" in English. Explore the otherworldly landscape on foot or horseback, and capture stunning photos against the backdrop of the dramatic rock formations and the vast ocean.

Regina Rica, Albayn

Nestled on a hilltop overlooking the majestic Mayon Volcano, Regina Rica is a pilgrimage site known for its towering statue of the Virgin Mary. Beyond its religious significance, the site offers panoramic views of the surrounding landscape, making it a perfect spot for nature lovers and photographers.

Kabayan Cove, Bataan

Craving a serene beach escape? Look no further than Kabayan Cove in Bataan. This hidden gem boasts pristine white sand, crystal-clear waters, and a laid-back atmosphere. Unlike the more crowded beaches in the region, Kabayan Cove offers a tranquil escape where you can relax, swim, and soak up the sun without the tourist hordes.
