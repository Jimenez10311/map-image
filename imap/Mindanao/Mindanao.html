Daku Island, Siargao

Often overshadowed by its famous neighbor Siargao Island, Daku Island offers a more secluded and peaceful getaway. With its pristine white sand beach, turquoise waters, and lush greenery, it's a paradise for beach bums and nature enthusiasts. Explore hidden coves, go island hopping, or simply relax under the palm trees with a good book.

Malamawi Island, Davao de Oro

This tiny island boasts a rich biodiversity and stunning natural beauty. Trek through the dense rainforest, discover hidden waterfalls, and relax on the secluded beach. You can also go snorkeling or diving and encounter a diverse marine life.

Tinuy-an Falls, Surigao del Sur

If you're looking for an adventure, Tinuy-an Falls is a must-visit. This three-tiered waterfall cascades down a lush canyon, creating a breathtaking spectacle.
